Velvet pants

Velvet pants are a type of pants that are made of velvet, a plush and luxurious fabric that is soft to the touch. Velvet pants are known for their rich, sumptuous texture and their elegant appearance. They are often worn for formal or special occasions, and can be found in a wide range of colors, from classic black and navy to more bold and vibrant shades. Velvet pants can be paired with a variety of tops, from dressy blouses to casual t-shirts, and can be dressed up with accessories such as a belt or a jacket. Velvet pants are a fashionable and chic option for those who want to add a touch of luxury to their wardrobe. Velvet is ever so chic and classy.




Cargo Pants

Cargo pants, also known as cargo trousers, are a type of pants that are designed with large, spacious pockets on the sides of the legs. These pockets are often used to carry a variety of items, such as tools, gear, or other small items that need to be easily accessible. Cargo pants are often associated with military and outdoor activities, and are known for their durability and practicality. They are typically made of a sturdy and durable fabric, such as cotton or a cotton blend, and have a relaxed and comfortable fit. Cargo pants are often worn by both men and women, and can be found in a wide range of colors, styles, and sizes.
