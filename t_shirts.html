Levis Henley T-Shirt

Henleys are a surefire way to lend a layer of nonchalant sophistication to your look. They are smart, comfortable and a piece of clothing that will make you look mature, and more likely to be taken seriously whether it is at work or on a date. Stand apart and opt for a henley with a simplistic placket like this pink number to give your tee look a modern makeover. A Henley t-shirt is a type of shirt that is similar in design to a traditional t-shirt, but features a button placket that runs down the front of the shirt. The placket typically has two or three buttons, and can be left open or closed, depending on the wearer's preference. Henley t-shirts are named after the English town of Henley-on-Thames, where they were first worn by rowers competing in the Henley Royal Regatta. Henley t-shirts are a versatile and comfortable garment that can be worn for a variety of occasions, from casual to semi-formal. They are often made of soft, breathable fabrics, such as cotton or cotton blends, and can be found in a wide range of colors and styles.
